Themes and Fonts

Customise the look and feel of ScriptureStudy.

Themes

Open Settings → Appearance to choose a theme. Available themes include:

  • Light — clean white background, suitable for bright environments
  • Warm — a soft cream tone easier on the eyes for long reading sessions
  • Sepia — a traditional book-like warm brown tint
  • Charcoal — a dark grey theme for low-light reading
  • Black — a true black OLED-style dark theme

Accent colours

The filigree accent colour is used throughout the interface for verse numbers, headings, icons and decorative elements. Five presets are available:

  • Pale Blue (default)
  • Pale Sage
  • Pale Gold
  • Pale Lavender
  • Blush Pink

The accent colour automatically adjusts its brightness to remain legible on both light and dark themes.

Font selection

Choose any font installed on your Mac for the Bible reading area and note editor. A font size slider lets you adjust the reading size independently. Changes apply immediately across all views.

Launch animation

ScriptureStudy shows a brief animated logo on launch. This can be disabled in Settings → Behaviour → Show launch animation.
