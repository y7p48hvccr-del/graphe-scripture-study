Dictionaries Tab

Strong's definitions and general dictionary lookups in one panel.

Two sections

The Dictionaries tab is split into two independent sections:

  • Strong's — shows the lexicon entry for the last word you tapped in the main Bible text. The picker above this section lets you choose which Strong's lexicon to use.
  • Dictionary — shows the definition of the last word you copied to the clipboard. The picker above this section lets you choose which plain dictionary to use (e.g. Webster's).

Strong's lookup

Tap any highlighted word in the Bible panel to look it up. The Strong's number, original word, transliteration and definition appear in the Strong's section automatically. ScriptureStudy detects whether you are in the Old or New Testament and uses the correct Hebrew or Greek entries.

Dictionary clipboard lookup

Copy any word — from anywhere on your Mac — and if a plain dictionary module is installed, its definition appears in the Dictionary section within half a second. This works across the entire system, not just within ScriptureStudy.

Changing modules

Both pickers update immediately when you change selection — the current word or Strong's number is re-looked up in the newly selected module without requiring you to tap or copy again.

Distinguishing module types

In the Library, Strong's modules show a purple H□ icon. Plain dictionaries show a green book icon. ScriptureStudy detects the type automatically from the is_strong field in each module's info table.
