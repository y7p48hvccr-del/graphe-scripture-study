Notes Tab — Companion Panel

Read your notes alongside the Bible text without leaving the reading view.

Opening notes for a verse

When a verse has notes linked to it, a small note icon appears to the left of the verse number in the Bible panel. Tapping this icon switches the Companion panel to the Notes tab and displays all notes linked to that verse.

How notes are displayed

Each note appears in its own scrollable section. The first line of the note is displayed as a bold heading; the remaining content appears below it in the body area. The verse reference is shown in the accent colour beneath each note. If a verse has multiple notes, they are stacked vertically, each in its own independently scrollable panel divided equally within the available height.

Read-only view

Notes in the Companion panel are read-only. To edit a note, switch to the Organizer tab and select it there.

Notes cleared on navigation

The Notes tab clears automatically when you navigate to a different chapter or book, ready to show notes for the new passage.
