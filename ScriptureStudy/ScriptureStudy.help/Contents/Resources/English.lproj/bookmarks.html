Bookmarks

Save chapters for quick return.

Adding a bookmark

Click the thin ribbon at the top-right of the Bible reading area. It turns solid red to confirm the bookmark is saved. The current book and chapter are recorded along with the date.

Removing a bookmark

Click the ribbon again on a bookmarked chapter — it returns to its inactive light-grey state and the bookmark is removed.

Viewing and navigating bookmarks

All your bookmarks appear in the Organizer tab in the sidebar at the top of the page, above your notes. Each entry shows the book and chapter name, the date it was saved, and an arrow button to navigate directly to that passage. Right-click any bookmark to delete it.

iCloud sync

Bookmarks are stored in iCloud's key-value store and sync automatically across all your Macs running ScriptureStudy.
