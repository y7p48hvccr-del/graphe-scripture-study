The Bible Panel

The main reading area where you navigate and read your Bible text.

Sidebar controls

The left sidebar contains four menus for navigating your text:

  • Bible — selects your active translation from installed modules
  • Book — selects the Bible book
  • Chapter — selects the chapter within the book

Each menu shows the current selection and opens a scrollable list of options when clicked.

Reading the text

The main reading area shows the chapter text with verse numbers on the left. If a Strong's lexicon is installed and selected, individual words appear as interactive tokens you can tap to look up their definition.

Verse selection

Tap a verse number to select that verse — it highlights and the Companion panel scrolls to the matching verse in whichever tab is active. Tap the same verse number again to deselect it.

Long-press a verse number (hold for 0.4 seconds) to create a new note linked to that verse. The Organizer tab opens automatically.

Note indicators

If a verse has notes linked to it, a small note icon (📝) appears to the left of the verse number. Tapping it opens the Notes tab in the Companion panel showing all notes for that verse in read-only view.

The bookmark ribbon

A thin ribbon appears at the top-right corner of the reading area. Click it to bookmark the current chapter — it turns red when active. Click again to remove the bookmark. Bookmarks are saved to iCloud and appear in the Organizer tab.

Scroll synchronisation

When the Companion panel is showing the Comparison tab, it scrolls in synchronisation with the main Bible panel. As you scroll through the chapter, the comparison translation tracks your position automatically.

Hint bar

When a Strong's lexicon is active, a subtle hint appears below the sidebar reminding you of the available gestures: tap a word for its Strong's definition, tap a verse number to select it, long-press to create a note.
