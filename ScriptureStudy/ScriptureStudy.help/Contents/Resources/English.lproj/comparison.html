Comparison Tab

Read two Bible translations side by side.

Selecting a comparison Bible

In the Companion panel, switch to the Comparison tab. A picker in the module bar at the top of the panel lets you choose any installed Bible translation. Select one and its text loads immediately for the same book and chapter you are reading in the main panel.

Scroll synchronisation

The comparison panel tracks your position in the main Bible automatically. As you scroll through the main text, the comparison translation scrolls to keep the same verses in view. Note that because different translations can vary in verse length and formatting, the sync is verse-based rather than pixel-perfect.

Verse highlighting

When you tap a verse number in the main panel, the corresponding verse in the comparison translation highlights and centres in view. This makes it easy to compare how a specific verse reads across both translations.

For the best experience, choose translations that use the same versification system (e.g. both KJV-based). Translations with significantly different verse numbering (such as some Psalms) may show minor sync offsets.
