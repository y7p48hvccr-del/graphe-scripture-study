Welcome to ScriptureStudy

A native macOS Bible study application built around the MyBible module format.

ScriptureStudy is designed to keep your study materials close together — your Bible text, parallel translations, Strong's lexicons, commentaries, dictionaries, and personal notes all available simultaneously in a clean, readable layout.

What you can do

  • Read any Bible translation in the MyBible .SQLite3 format
  • Compare translations side-by-side in the Companion panel
  • Tap any word to look up its Strong's Hebrew or Greek definition
  • Read commentaries and dictionaries alongside the text
  • Write notes linked to specific verses, chapters, or passages
  • Bookmark chapters and navigate your reading history
  • Search across all your installed modules
  • Generate AI-powered chapter summaries using Claude or Ollama

Where to begin

If this is your first time opening ScriptureStudy, start with Quick Start to get a Bible loaded and readable within a few minutes. Then explore the Bible Panel and Installing Modules when you are ready to expand your library.

Tip: ScriptureStudy uses the open MyBible module format. Thousands of free modules — Bibles, commentaries, dictionaries and lexicons — are available from the MyBible community online.
