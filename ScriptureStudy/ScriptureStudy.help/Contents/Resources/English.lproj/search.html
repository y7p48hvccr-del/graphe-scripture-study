Search

Find passages and words across your installed modules.

Opening Search

Click the Search tab in the main tab bar (magnifying glass icon).

Searching the Bible

Type any word or phrase into the search field and press Return. Results show the verse reference and the matching text, with your search term highlighted. Click any result to navigate to that passage in the Bible panel.

Search scope

Search runs against your currently selected Bible translation. To search a different translation, switch to it in the Bible panel first.

Searching notes

The search tab also searches through your saved notes. Note results appear in a separate section below Bible results, showing the note title and a preview of the matching content. Click a note result to open it in the Organizer.
