Privacy Policy

Graphē One™ ScriptureStudy Pro

Last updated: April 2026

Studio Graphē One ("we", "our", "us") built Graphē One™ ScriptureStudy Pro as a paid application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Do Not Collect

Graphē One does not collect, transmit or store any personal data. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics and no advertising. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your Mac only and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

Your app preferences and settings (stored in macOS UserDefaults), your notes and bookmarks (stored in your local Documents folder), your reading position and history (stored in UserDefaults), your choice of modules folder (stored as a security-scoped bookmark in UserDefaults).

AI Features

If you choose to use the optional AI features (chapter summaries or chat), your Bible passages may be sent to the AI provider you have configured — either a local Ollama installation on your own Mac, or Anthropic's API if you supply your own API key. In both cases, this is a direct connection between your device and the provider you choose. Studio Graphē One is not involved in or privy to this data exchange. Please refer to your chosen provider's privacy policy for details.

iCloud

Graphē One uses iCloud key-value storage to sync your preferences across your Apple devices. This data is governed by Apple's privacy policy. No personal information about you is included in this sync — only app settings.

Children's Privacy

Graphē One does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@graphe.one.
