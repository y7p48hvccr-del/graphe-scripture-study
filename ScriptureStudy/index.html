Features

Everything you need,
working together seamlessly

Graphē is built around the conviction that good tools should get out of the way. Every feature connects naturally to the next.

📖
Bible Reading

Hundreds of translations in dozens of languages, with history navigation, verse bookmarks and customisable themes.

🗺
Bible Maps

Interactive maps with hundreds of named locations. Tap a place name in the text and jump straight to the map.

📜
Strong's Lexicon

Tap any word to explore its original Greek or Hebrew meaning. Full interlinear support with morphology.

📝
Commentary

Read classic commentaries alongside the text in the companion panel, synced to your current verse.

🗒
Notes

Create notes linked directly to verses. Organise, search, and export your study notes effortlessly.

📄
PDF Books

A beautifully designed PDF reader with full-text search, bookmarks, and annotation support.

✨
AI Summaries

AI-powered chapter summaries powered by your choice of Ollama or Anthropic. Entirely optional.

🌍
Hundreds of Free Resources

Bibles, commentaries, lexicons, devotionals and dictionaries — all free via the MyBible module library.
