Devotional

Daily devotional reading

Overview

The Devotional tab provides daily devotional readings from installed devotional modules. It opens automatically to today's entry each time you visit.

Module picker

A picker in the header bar lets you choose which devotional module to read. Installed devotionals include works such as Spurgeon's Morning by Morning, Moody's devotionals, and multi-year reading plans like Spurgeon's Interpreter. All installed devotional modules appear in this picker.

Day navigation

Use the ‹ and › arrows to move between days. The centre button shows the current day number and returns to today's entry when tapped.

Devotionals are numbered by day (Day 1–365) rather than by calendar date. The day number corresponds to the day of the year — Day 1 is 1st January.

Bible reference links

Scripture references within the devotional text are tappable links. Tapping one navigates the main Bible panel to that passage — the devotional remains visible so you can read both together.

Installing devotional modules

Copy devotional SQLite files (those containing a devotions table) into your modules folder and refresh the Library. They appear automatically in the Devotional picker and in the Devotionals section of the Library.
