Commentary

Verse and chapter commentary

Opening commentary

Switch the companion panel to the Commentary tab. Select a commentary module from the picker at the top. Commentary entries for the current chapter load automatically.

Navigation

The commentary panel scrolls independently from the main Bible panel. The currently selected verse is highlighted in the commentary. As you navigate chapters in the main Bible panel, the commentary updates to match.

Installing commentaries

Copy commentary SQLite files into your modules folder and refresh the Library. They appear in the Commentaries section. Multiple commentaries can be installed — use the picker to switch between them.
