Dictionaries & Lexicons

Word definitions and original language lookup

Dictionaries tab

The Dictionaries tab in the companion panel provides three separate pickers — one for your cross-reference module, one for your Strong's lexicon, and one for your dictionary. Each can be set independently.

Looking up words

Type any word or Strong's number into the search field to look it up. Dictionary entries display the word, definition and any related entries.

Strong's lookup

Tap a word in a Strong's-tagged Bible to look it up automatically. The Dictionaries tab switches to show the lexicon entry. See Strong's Numbers.

Installing modules

Dictionary modules (containing a dictionary table with is_strong=false) appear in the Dictionaries section of the Library. Lexicon modules (is_strong=true) appear in the Lexicons section.
