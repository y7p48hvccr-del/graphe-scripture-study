Themes & Fonts

Customising the reading experience

Themes

Four themes are available in Settings:

ThemeDescription
LightClean white background with dark text
SepiaWarm neutral parchment — easy on the eyes for long reading
BlushSoft pale pink background
Light GreyCool light grey — reduces contrast slightly
CharcoalDark background with light text for night reading

Accent colour

The accent colour controls verse numbers, links, icons and highlighted elements throughout the app. Choose from a range of colour families in Settings.

Font

Select from a curated list of reading-friendly fonts. Font size can be adjusted with the slider. Changes apply immediately to the Bible text and companion panels.

Texture

An optional subtle paper grain texture can be applied over the background. Adjust the intensity with the slider in Settings.
