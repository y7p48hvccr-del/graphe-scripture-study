Graphē ScriptureStudy Pro

A personal Bible study environment for Mac

Graphē ScriptureStudy Pro brings together Bible translation, cross-references, commentaries, lexicons, devotionals and personal notes in one focused workspace. All modules are MyBible-format SQLite files stored in a folder of your choosing.
