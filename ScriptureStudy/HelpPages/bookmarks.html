Bookmarks

Saving and revisiting passages

Adding a bookmark

Click the thin ribbon in the top-right corner of the Bible reading area to bookmark the current chapter. The ribbon turns red when active. Click it again to remove the bookmark.

Viewing bookmarks

Bookmarks appear in the left panel of the Books tab in a scrollable list. Each bookmark shows the book and chapter name and the date it was saved. Click the arrow button to navigate directly to that passage in the Bible panel.

Deleting a bookmark

Right-click any bookmark in the Books tab panel and choose Delete Bookmark. You can also remove a bookmark by clicking the ribbon in the Bible panel while on that chapter.

Sync

Bookmarks are stored in iCloud Key-Value storage and sync automatically across your devices when iCloud is active.
