Bookmarks

Saving and revisiting passages

Adding a bookmark

Click the thin ribbon in the top-right corner of the Bible reading area to bookmark the current chapter. The ribbon turns red when a chapter is bookmarked. Click it again to remove the bookmark.

Viewing bookmarks

Bookmarks appear in the lower-left panel of the Organizer tab in a scrollable list. Each bookmark shows the book and chapter. Tap the arrow to navigate there. Right-click a bookmark to delete it.

Sync

Bookmarks are stored in iCloud Key-Value storage and sync automatically across your devices.
