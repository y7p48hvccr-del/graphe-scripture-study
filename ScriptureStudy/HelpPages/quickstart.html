Quick Start

Get reading in three steps

Step 1 — Choose your modules folder

Graphē ScriptureStudy Pro reads MyBible-format SQLite files from a folder on your Mac or iCloud Drive. Go to the Archives tab and click Choose Modules Folder. Point it to wherever you keep your .SQLite3 module files. The app scans the folder and detects all installed modules automatically.

Your modules folder can be anywhere — a local folder, iCloud Drive, or a Dropbox/OneDrive folder. The app just needs read access.

Step 2 — Select a Bible

Once modules are loaded, go to the Archives tab and find your Bible in the Bibles section. Click Use to make it your active Bible. Then click the Bible tab to start reading.

Step 3 — Start reading

Use the picker bar at the top of the Bible panel to choose your Bible version, book and chapter. Tap any verse number to select it — this loads commentary and cross-references in the Companion Panel automatically.

What to do next

  • Long-press a verse number to create a note anchored to that verse
  • Tap any word to look up its Strong's number (if your Bible has Strong's tagging)
  • Open the Companion Panel to see cross-references, commentary, interlinear or a second Bible version
  • Visit the Organizer tab to set up a reading plan and add people to your portrait panel
  • Visit the Devotional tab to read your daily devotional
  • Visit the Books tab to read EPUB and PDF books alongside your study
  • Go to Settings to choose your preferred font, theme and AI engine
Modules are available free from mybible.zone — hundreds of Bible translations, commentaries, lexicons and devotionals in many languages.
