Bookmarks

Your saved chapters, always close at hand.

Where bookmarks live

Bookmarks are displayed in the left panel of the Books tab. They appear in a scrollable list beneath the search bar, showing the book name, chapter, and date saved.

Navigating to a bookmark

Click the arrow button (→) next to any bookmark to navigate directly to that chapter in the Bible panel.

Adding a bookmark

Click the thin ribbon in the top-right corner of the Bible reading area to bookmark the current chapter. The ribbon turns red when active.

Deleting a bookmark

Right-click any bookmark and choose Delete Bookmark from the context menu. You can also remove it by clicking the ribbon again while reading that chapter.
