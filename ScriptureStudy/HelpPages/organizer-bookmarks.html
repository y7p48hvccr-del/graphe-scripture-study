Bookmarks in the Organizer

All your saved chapters in one place.

Viewing bookmarks

The Organizer tab shows your bookmarks at the top of the sidebar, above the notes list. Each bookmark shows the book and chapter name and the date it was saved.

Navigating to a bookmark

Click the arrow button (→) next to any bookmark to navigate directly to that chapter in the Bible panel.

Deleting a bookmark

Right-click any bookmark and choose Delete Bookmark from the context menu. You can also delete a bookmark by clicking the ribbon in the Bible panel while on that chapter.
