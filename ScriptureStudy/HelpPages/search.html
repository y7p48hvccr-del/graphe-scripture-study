Search

Searching across Bible modules

Basic search

Go to the Search tab. Type your search term and press Return. Results show matching verses across your active Bible module with the matching words highlighted.

Navigating results

Tap any result to navigate the main Bible panel to that verse.

Tips

  • Search is case-insensitive
  • Use multiple words to find phrases
  • Short words like "the" or "and" may return too many results to be useful
