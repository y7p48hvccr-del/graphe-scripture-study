Bible Panel

Your main reading window

Navigation bar

A compact picker bar sits above the Bible text with three menus — Bible, Book and Chapter. Click any of them to change your position. The Bible menu lists all visible Bible modules from your library.

Verse selection

Tap a verse number to select it. The verse highlights and the Companion Panel switches to the Commentary tab, scrolling to that verse automatically. Cross-references also load in the background. Tap the same verse number again to deselect.

Creating a note

Long-press a verse number (hold for about 0.4 seconds) to create a new note linked to that verse. The Organizer tab opens automatically with the note editor ready.

Note indicators

If a verse has notes linked to it, a small note icon appears to the right of the verse. Tapping it opens the Notes tab in the Companion Panel showing all notes for that verse.

Strong's interlinear words

If a Strong's-tagged Bible is loaded, individual words appear as interactive tokens. Tap a word to look up its Strong's entry in the Dictionaries tab of the Companion Panel.

The bookmark ribbon

A thin ribbon appears in the top-right corner of the reading area. Click it to bookmark the current chapter — it turns red when active. Click again to remove the bookmark. Bookmarks are saved to iCloud and appear in the Organizer tab.

Scroll synchronisation

When the Companion Panel is showing Commentary, it scrolls in sync with the main Bible panel as you read through the chapter.
