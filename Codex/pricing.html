Graphē One Codex

Pricing

Choose the level of Codex that fits your reading practice. Codex is designed as a high-trust reading environment for complex texts: begin with a serious core reader, add deeper Bible study tools when needed, and scale into advanced research and professional workflows over time.

Codex Core

A focused reading environment for structured texts, EPUBs, and PDFs.

  • Persistent local-first library
  • Built-in Bible included as a permanent volume
  • EPUB and PDF import
  • Bookmarks and reading position
  • Structured navigation and contents
  • Offline reading
  • Privacy-first foundations

Codex Bible

Deeper Bible study and reference tools within the Codex environment.

  • Everything in Codex Core
  • Lexical lookup
  • Cross references
  • Commentary and study overlays
  • Verse-level notes and highlights
  • Advanced canonical navigation
  • Richer Bible reference workflows

Codex Professional

Advanced research and professional reading workflows for large, demanding document collections.

  • Everything in lower tiers
  • Advanced annotation system
  • Notes, tags, notebooks, and research collections
  • Cross-document linking
  • Parallel and comparative reading
  • Advanced search across the library
  • Citation, excerpt, and export tools
  • Structured navigation for very large works
  • Richer sync for workspaces and metadata
  • Professional workflows for legal, academic, technical, governmental, and institutional reading

Comparison

Core for serious reading. Bible for deeper study. Professional for intensive research and large-document work.

Incremental Release

Codex is expected to be introduced incrementally. The initial release may begin with Codex Core and Codex Bible, with Codex Professional developing later as a broader research and institutional layer.

Approach

Codex Core should feel complete. Codex Bible should deepen study and reference work without redefining the app’s identity. Codex Professional should extend the same reading environment into a full research workspace for large, structured collections.
