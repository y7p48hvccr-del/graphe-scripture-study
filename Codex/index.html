Built for serious reading.

Graphē One Codex is designed for readers who need more than a simple file viewer. It brings structured texts, EPUB documents, and PDF documents into one coherent reading environment, with long-form reading, durable navigation, and large-document handling in mind.

Structured Text Reading

Navigate works through sections, subsections, and passage-aware layouts rather than flat page flow alone.

EPUB and PDF Support

Bring imported publications into the same library alongside native structured texts.

Persistent Library

Your documents, bookmarks, and reading position remain available across sessions.

Long-Document Navigation

Prepared for dense internal structure, nested contents, linked references, and metadata-rich reading.

Cross-Platform Direction

Designed with iPhone, iPad, and Mac in mind, with cloud-connected workflows planned.

Privacy-First Foundations

Built around local-first reading, durable state, and a high-trust approach to personal libraries.

Choose Your Level

Codex Professional
