Features

Everything you need, working together seamlessly

Codex is a dedicated reader for the large book format — fast when the work is enormous, precise when the text is dense, and quiet enough to disappear when you just want to read.

Massive Documents

Fast, fluid handling of thousand-page PDFs and multi-volume works. Where other readers stall, Codex keeps pace.

EPUB & PDF

First-class support for both formats, with the same powerful navigation and search tools across them.

Nested & Collapsible TOC

Deeply nested tables of contents that collapse and expand at every level. Navigate vast structured works without ever losing your place.

Full-Text Search

Near-instant search across entire volumes, with ranked results and live previews of each match in context.

Annotations

Highlight, underline, and annotate passages. Your marks stay with you across iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

Beautiful Typography

Carefully chosen fonts, adjustable size, line height, margins, and day-and-night themes. Read your way.

Cross-References

Tap any reference to see its target in a popover, or jump to it in a single step. Built for richly cross-referenced texts and critical editions.

Lexicon Popovers

Tap any word to open a dictionary, lexicon, or glossary entry inline. Explore original-language roots without ever leaving the page.
