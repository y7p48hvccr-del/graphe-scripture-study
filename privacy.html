Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Graphē Scripture Study Pro is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store or sell your personal data. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Data we do not collect

Graphē does not collect any personal information. We do not have accounts, we do not require registration, and we do not track your usage, location or behaviour.

Data stored on your device

All data created within the app — including notes, bookmarks, highlights, settings and module files — is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to enable iCloud sync.

iCloud sync

If you choose to enable iCloud sync, your notes, bookmarks and settings are stored in your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit service. This data is governed by Apple's privacy policy and is accessible only to you. We have no access to your iCloud data.

AI features

Graphē includes an optional AI study assistant. If you use the local Ollama option, all processing happens entirely on your device and no data is sent anywhere. If you choose to use the Anthropic Claude API option, your queries are sent to Anthropic's servers in accordance with Anthropic's privacy policy. No queries are stored or associated with your identity by us.

Third-party content

The Discover feature in the Books section loads web pages from third-party sites such as Monergism, CCEL and others. When you browse these sites within the app, you are subject to those sites' own privacy policies. Graphē does not intercept or store any data from these browsing sessions.

Analytics

Graphē does not use any analytics, crash reporting or advertising services that collect personal data.

Children

Graphē does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, please use the support page.
